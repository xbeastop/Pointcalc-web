FAQ

Common questions

Quick answers about supported games, the AI feature and pricing.

What games does PointCalc support?
PointCalc supports BGMI, PUBG Mobile and Free Fire — the three biggest battle royale titles in the tournament/scrim community. You upload lobby and result screens; PointCalc then generates the points table, warhead, top fraggers (MVP), slot list, team poster and certificates from that data.
How does the AI screenshot extraction work?
Upload your lobby and result screens from a match. PointCalc's AI reads the placements, kills and player stats directly off the images and populates your points table, warhead, top fraggers (MVP), slot list, team poster and certificates automatically. You can review and edit any value before publishing — accuracy holds up across most result-screen layouts.
Is PointCalc free?
Yes — the app is free to download and the core features work without a subscription. Premium templates and AI screenshot extraction are available on optional paid tiers, starting at ₹29/week for Premium and ₹150/week for Premium + AI.
Can I customize designs with my own branding?
Yes. You can adjust colors, swap the background image and add as many custom logos as you need. For an entirely custom design, reach out to PointCalc on WhatsApp or Instagram and we'll build it for you.

Meet xbeastop

xbeastop

Founder · Developer · Made in Kerala

I started PointCalc in September 2021. I was a PUBG player back then, running scrims with my friends, and the worst part of tournament day was never the matches — it was the after-math. Hours of manual point calculations, then hours more in Photoshop putting the result graphics together. A friend in our circle was doing all that grunt work for us. I covered for him one week, and after two days I broke. There had to be a better way.

Read more Show less

I was a BCA student who only knew C at the time. I watched a 14-hour Android tutorial, started building the day after, and had a working prototype inside a week. The friend I built it for wasn't really satisfied with it — so I quietly used it myself, then shipped versions to two or three other orgs I knew. That's when it clicked: instead of one-off builds for each org, ship one app that any organizer can customize. Play Store publishing wasn't free, so my college friends pitched in to cover the developer fee. PointCalc's been live ever since.

These days I'm an Android developer at Wrench Solutions in Kochi by day, and PointCalc is still my own project on the side — now at 100K+ downloads, with AI screenshot extraction and an iOS build. For partnerships, custom designs or just to say hi, reach me on WhatsApp or Instagram.
