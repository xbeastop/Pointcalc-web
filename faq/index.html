FAQ

Questions, answered.

Everything we get asked about PointCalc — supported games, the AI feature, pricing and more.

What is PointCalc?
PointCalc is an AI-powered tournament toolkit for esports organizers. Built for BGMI, PUBG Mobile and Free Fire, it reads lobby and result screenshots to auto-extract placements and kills — then generates publish-ready point tables, top fragger cards, MVP and warhead stats, team posters, slot lists and certificates. Available on iOS and Android, with 100K+ downloads.
Which games does PointCalc support?
PointCalc supports BGMI (Battlegrounds Mobile India), PUBG Mobile and Free Fire. The AI screenshot extraction reads lobby and result screens from all three titles — top fragger, MVP and warhead stats are then generated automatically from that match data.
How does the AI screenshot extraction work?
Upload a result screenshot from your match. PointCalc's AI reads placement, kill counts and player stats directly off the image and populates your point table automatically. You can review and edit any value before publishing. The Premium + AI tier also supports bulk match processing — drop in several matches at once and PointCalc compiles the standings.
Is PointCalc free?
Yes — PointCalc is free to download and the core features work without a subscription. Two optional paid tiers unlock more:

• Premium — premium design templates, priority support. From ₹29/week, ₹69/month, ₹200/quarter, ₹700/year.
• Premium + AI — everything in Premium plus AI screenshot extraction and bulk processing. From ₹150/week, ₹350/month, ₹1000/quarter, ₹3500/year.
Can I customize designs with my own branding?
Yes. You can adjust colors, swap the background image and add as many custom logos as you need. For an entirely custom design, reach out to PointCalc on WhatsApp or Instagram and we'll build it for you.
Does PointCalc work without internet?
Core point table calculation, manual data entry and design exports work offline. AI screenshot extraction requires an internet connection because the recognition runs in the cloud.
Can multiple admins collaborate on the same tournament?
Yes. Tournament data can be shared with co-admins so multiple people can update stats, post results and publish designs in parallel during a long event.
What's the difference between Premium and Premium + AI?
Premium unlocks premium design templates, removes restrictions on core features and gives priority support. Premium + AI adds everything in Premium plus AI screenshot extraction (lobby + result screens), auto-generated top fragger / MVP / warhead stats, bulk match processing and earliest access to new AI features.
Who built PointCalc?
PointCalc is built by xbeastop (Amal Manoj), an indie developer from India. It started as a tool for the local BGMI scrim community and grew to 100,000+ downloads across iOS and Android.
